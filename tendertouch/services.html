TENDER TOUCH

SMALL ANIMAL HOSPITAL

1. Preventive and Wellness Care

At our clinic, we prioritize the long-term health and well-being of your pets. Our preventive and wellness services encompass comprehensive wellness exams, tailored vaccinations, and thorough parasite prevention. We cater to all life stages, offering specialized care for the young and vibrant puppies and kittens, as well as compassionate senior care to ensure your pet's golden years are comfortable. Keeping your pets healthy and happy is our top priority.

Learn More

2. Diagnostic and Therapeutic Services

We are equipped with advanced diagnostic tools and offer a wide range of therapeutic services to accurately identify and treat the unique challenges your pet may face. Our state-of-the-art radiology (X-rays) and in-house laboratory tests allow for rapid and accurate diagnosis. We also provide behavioral medicine and nutritional counseling to address all aspects of your pet's health and well-being, ensuring a holistic approach to their care.

Schedule Now

3. Advanced Treatment and Pain Management

We offer innovative treatment options including acupuncture and laser therapy, which are effective for a variety of conditions, enhancing healing and providing pain relief. Our approach to pain management is comprehensive, utilizing the latest techniques and therapies to ensure your pet's comfort and improve their quality of life. Our trained veterinarians tailor treatments to the specific needs of your pet, whether they're recovering from surgery or managing chronic conditions.

Learn More

4. Surgical and Dental Services

Our surgical suite is equipped to handle a wide range of procedures, ensuring that your pet receives the best possible care in times of need. From routine surgeries to more complex operations, we maintain the highest standards of safety and care. We also provide comprehensive dental care, including cleaning, diagnostics, and surgeries, to maintain your pet's oral health. Every procedure is conducted under carefully controlled anesthesia to ensure your pet's comfort and safety.

Schedule Now

5. Additional Services

"Our clinic goes beyond traditional veterinary care by offering additional services that make managing your pet's health convenient. We offer telemedicine consultations to provide care at your convenience, a fully stocked pharmacy for all your pet's medication needs, and microchipping to ensure your pet's safety. We also provide compassionate hospice and euthanasia services, respecting the dignity of your pet's final days. Additionally, our cat boarding facilities ensure your pet's well-being and comfort while you're away.
